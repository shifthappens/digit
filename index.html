Welcome to the Prague Treasure Hunt mini-game

Caution! this game is in ALPHA state, meaning very basic, under development and may contain bugs. Please contact Coen, Adrian, Sergii or Linnea if you experience technical difficulties.

Step 1: allow use of your location (required)

This game requires the use of your geolocation. Please allow your location to be used by clicking this button.

Step 2: allow us to save your game (optional)

To continue where you left off between browser sessions, allow us to save your progress locally. Nothing will be sent to any server. Not clicking the button means you have to start over with every page refresh or browser session.

Step 3: How to play

Get within 10 meters of a marker on the map to activate it or catch it. The marker will turn green and the counter will advance by one in the top right corner to indicate your progress. Tap the marker to open a popup, read about the location and complete the task in the description.

The blue circle around the crosshair indicates the accuracy of your GPS signal.

Step 4: How to win

Collect all your answers (mention the mural title in the popup and the answer to the question) and submit them latest Sunday June 2nd at noon to useitgame@coen.at. The team / person with the most points (based on number of correct answers, amount of items collected and/or originality of the answers given), will win a small prize. Winners announced on Sunday June 2nd around 20:00 during the awards.
